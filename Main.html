Main

Enver Creek Secondary School is a public High school in Surrey. Enver Creek is a part of School district 36 Surrey. The principal of the school is Mr.Kilpatrick and the 2 vice principals are Mr.Magnusson and Mrs.Sadra.The school was founded in 1997. Enver Creek offers scholarships for honour roll students and students who excell in school and participate in many volunteer activities and after school activities.
