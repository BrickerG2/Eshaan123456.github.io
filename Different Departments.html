Different Departments

Enver Creek's departments are basically the school's classes. Enver Creek's main academics are: Mathematics, Science, Socials, English, French and PE. There are many elctives but the main one's are woodwork, drama, computers and photography and dance. Electives give students an opportunity to choose whatever interests them the most and what they think they will enjoy the most.
