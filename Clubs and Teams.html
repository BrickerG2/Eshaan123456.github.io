Clubs And Teams

Enver Creek offers many sport teams and clubs for all students.Some of the sport teams are: basketball, soccer, volleyball, badminton, football, cross country, golf, ice hockey, ball hockey, rugby, and freestyle wrestling, anybody can join these teams and the coaches are great and help you learn the techniques for the sport you want to play. Enver Creek also has many clubs to choose from like a bhangra team, dance team, grad council, fly fishing, green team, math club, anti-bullying, newspaper, robotics, science-club, snowboard and ski club and weight-training.

Basketball

Basketball is the school's most popular sport and has a team for all grades with some playing on the same team.For example, grade 8 have their own team while 9-10 play on the same team which is juniours and the senior team is grade 11-12.

Soccer

Soccer is also one of the most school's most popular teams and arguably the most popular sport in the world.Grades 8-10 play on 1 team and the senior team consists of the grades 11-12.
